Frequently Asked Questions

常见问题

What exactly am I purchasing?
You are purchasing prepaid API credits for the ZenRouter service. Credits are added to your account balance immediately after payment and are consumed as you make API requests, priced per token according to the model used. Each purchase is a one-time top-up — there is no subscription and no automatic recurring charge.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a 7-day refund for unused credits. If you are not satisfied, contact us within 7 days of purchase and we will refund the unused portion of your credit balance to your original payment method. Credits that have already been consumed by API usage are non-refundable. See our full Refund Policy for details.
Is this a subscription? Will I be charged again?
No. All purchases are one-time top-ups. You will never be charged automatically. When your balance runs low, you can choose to top up again manually — entirely at your discretion.
Do credits expire?
Credits remain valid for 12 months from the date of purchase. We will notify you by email before any credits are due to expire.
Which models are supported?
We aggregate leading large language models from multiple providers behind a single OpenAI-compatible API, including GPT-series, Claude-series, Gemini-series and popular open-weight models. The current model list and per-token pricing are published in your account dashboard.
How is usage calculated?
Usage is metered per request based on input and output tokens, at the per-model rates shown in your dashboard. Every request is itemized in your usage logs, so you can audit exactly where your credits go.
How do I get support?
Email us at support@zenrouter.io. We respond to all billing and refund inquiries within 2 business days.
