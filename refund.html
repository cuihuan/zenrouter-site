Refund Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

Summary: We offer a 7-day refund for unused credits. Request a refund within 7 days of purchase and we will return the unused portion of that purchase to your original payment method. Credits already consumed by API usage are non-refundable.

1. Scope

This Refund Policy applies to all purchases of prepaid API credits for the ZenRouter service operated by ZenRouter. All credit purchases are one-time top-ups; the Service does not sell subscriptions and never charges on a recurring basis.

2. 7-Day Refund Window

  • You may request a refund within 7 calendar days of the purchase date.
  • The refundable amount is the unused portion of the credits from that purchase. For example, if you purchased $30 in credits and consumed $4 in API usage, the refundable amount is $26.
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
  • After the 7-day window, credit purchases are non-refundable, except where required by applicable consumer protection law.

3. Non-Refundable Items

  • Credits that have already been consumed by API requests.
  • Credits that have expired (credits are valid for 12 months from purchase).
  • Purchases associated with accounts terminated for violation of our Terms of Service (e.g., fraud or abuse).

4. How to Request a Refund

  1. Email support@zenrouter.io from the email address associated with your account.
  2. Include your order ID or transaction reference and the email address on the account.
  3. We will acknowledge your request within 2 business days and, once approved, issue the refund within 5–10 business days. The exact time for funds to appear depends on your bank or card issuer.

5. Failed or Duplicate Charges

If you were charged but did not receive credits, or were charged more than once for the same purchase, contact us with the transaction details and we will correct the balance or issue a full refund of the erroneous charge.

6. Chargebacks

If you believe a charge is incorrect, please contact us first — we resolve most billing issues quickly and without dispute. Initiating a chargeback for credits that have already been consumed may result in suspension of the associated account while the dispute is investigated.

7. Statutory Rights

Nothing in this policy limits any rights you may have under mandatory consumer protection laws in your jurisdiction.

8. Contact

For all refund and billing inquiries, contact ZenRouter at support@zenrouter.io.
