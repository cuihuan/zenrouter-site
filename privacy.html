Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ZenRouter ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use the ZenRouter service (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: email address, display name, and hashed password when you register.
  • Billing information: purchase history, credit balance, and transaction identifiers. Payment card details are processed by our third-party payment provider and are not stored on our servers.
  • Usage data: API request metadata such as timestamps, model selected, token counts, latency, status codes, and the API key used. This data powers metering, billing, rate limiting, and abuse prevention.
  • Request content: prompts and completions transit our systems in order to be routed to model providers. We do not use your API content to train models. Request bodies may be retained for a limited period (up to 30 days) solely for debugging and abuse investigation, after which they are deleted.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information collected when you visit our website or dashboard.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Service, including routing requests to model providers.
  • To meter usage, process payments, and maintain accurate billing records.
  • To detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • To respond to support requests and send service-related notices (e.g., low balance, credit expiry).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell personal information, and we do not use customer API content for advertising or model training.

3. Sharing of Information

  • Model providers: the content of your API requests is forwarded to the third-party model provider you select, as necessary to fulfill the request.
  • Payment processors: billing details required to complete transactions are shared with our payment provider.
  • Infrastructure vendors: hosting, logging, and email providers that process data on our behalf under data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements: we may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

4. Data Retention

Account and billing records are retained for as long as your account is active and as required for tax and accounting purposes. API request metadata is retained for up to 12 months. Request content, where retained for debugging, is deleted within 30 days. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time.

5. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit (TLS), encryption at rest for sensitive data, access controls, and audit logging. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. International Transfers

The Service is operated from multiple regions, and your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as standard contractual clauses for cross-border transfers.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@zenrouter.io. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

8. Cookies

Our website and dashboard use strictly necessary cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or an in-dashboard notice before taking effect.

11. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact ZenRouter at support@zenrouter.io.
